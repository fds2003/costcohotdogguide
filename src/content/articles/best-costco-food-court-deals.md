# Best Costco Food Court Deals in 2026

The Costco Food Court has earned legendary status among warehouse shoppers. While prices have crept up on some items over the years, several deals remain unbeatable in the fast-casual dining landscape.

## The Iconic: Hot Dog + Soda Combo ($1.50)

Since 1985, the price of a Costco hot dog and 20 oz soda has remained **$1.50**. Adjusted for inflation, this combo should cost over $4 today. The 100% all-beef frankfurter comes in a satisfyingly large bun with your choice of condiments.

**Value verdict:** Unbeatable. No competitor comes close at this price point.

## The Hidden Gem: Pizza Slice ($1.99)

At nearly a quarter of the pizza per slice, the $1.99 cheese or pepperoni slice delivers incredible value. Each 18-inch pizza is cut into 6 massive slices, making this one of the largest single slices available anywhere.

**Value verdict:** Excellent. Competing chains charge $3-5 for smaller slices.

## The Family Feeder: Whole Pizza ($9.95)

An entire 18-inch pizza for under $10 feeds 4-6 people comfortably. Compare this to $15-20 at major chains for a smaller pie.

**Value verdict:** Outstanding for families and groups.

## The Protein Powerhouse: Rotisserie Chicken ($4.99)

While technically not in the food court seating area, the $4.99 rotisserie chicken is a Costco institution. At 3 pounds, this bird delivers more protein per dollar than almost any prepared food in America.

**Value verdict:** Industry-leading. Competitors charge the same for 25% less chicken.

## Seasonal Surprises

Keep an eye out for seasonal items that rotate through the menu:
- **Acai Bowl** ($4.99) — A relatively healthy option with fresh fruit
- **Turkey & Swiss Sandwich** — Available at select locations
- **BBQ Beef Brisket Sandwich** — Regional specialty

## Pro Tips for Food Court Savings

1. **No membership required** — In most locations, you can visit the food court without a Costco membership
2. **Order the whole pizza** — If feeding 3+ people, the whole pie is significantly cheaper per slice
3. **Check for combo deals** — Some locations offer drink + slice combos
4. **Visit at off-peak hours** — Lines can exceed 15 minutes on weekends between 12-2 PM

## The Bottom Line

The Costco Food Court isn't just cheap—it's aggressively, almost defiantly cheap. The $1.50 hot dog combo isn't a loss leader anymore; it's a statement about what Costco believes customer loyalty is worth.

For shoppers who already make the trek to the warehouse, the food court transforms a routine errand into a genuinely enjoyable (and wallet-friendly) meal experience.
